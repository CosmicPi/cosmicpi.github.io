The cosmic ray detector on your desktop

The Cosmic Pi project is currently on pause! Unfortunately the great electronics shortage and the war in Ukraine have severly impacted the supply chain for building our detector design. We are currently thinking about what to do next to overcome these obstacles.

The Cosmic Pi project aims to build the world's largest open source distributed cosmic ray telescope. You can be a part of the project, by becoming a Cosmic Pixel!

Read more about the project on our blog, or go straight to our prototype design.
